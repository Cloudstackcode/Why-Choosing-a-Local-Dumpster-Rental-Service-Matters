Tips for a Smooth Dumpster Rental Experience

To help make your rental go smoothly, here are a few tips we always share with new customers:

  • Clear a Drop-Off Space: Make sure there’s enough room for the dumpster and the delivery truck. A clear driveway works best.
  • Load Efficiently: Start with flat items and heavier debris on the bottom, then layer lighter materials on top.
  • Avoid Overfilling: Don’t stack waste above the sides. This can create safety hazards and prevent us from hauling it away.
  • Check Restrictions: Never throw away prohibited items like paint, batteries, or appliances with freon. Ask us if you’re unsure.

We’ll walk you through everything when you book, but keeping these tips in mind helps make your project stress-free.
